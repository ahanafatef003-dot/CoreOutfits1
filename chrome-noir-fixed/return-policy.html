We want you to love your CORE OUTFITS pieces. If something isn't right, here's how our return process works.

Eligibility

Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging with tags attached. Returns must be initiated within 3 days of receiving your order.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Items marked as final sale
  • Accessories such as bandanas and gloves (for hygiene reasons)

How to Request a Return

Contact us via WhatsApp with your order ID and reason for return. Our team will guide you through the next steps.

Refunds

Approved refunds for bKash payments will be processed to the original payment number. Cash on Delivery orders may be eligible for store credit or exchange.
